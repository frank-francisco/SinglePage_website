More About Uniccus

Uniccus is a student targeted mobile application, aiming to make life in colleges and univerities easier


Uniccus is a free to download app.
It was built with the spirit of making Tanzanian college and university students' life easier, from the way they interact to the way they study. With this in mind we managed to come up with a super fast, super lite and super secure app. With this app students can easily socialise, make realtime announcements and study remotely through online classes.
Uniccus can help you reach your voice out to your fellow students with one click.
